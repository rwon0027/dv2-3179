Tobacco Taxes and Their Impact on Health

Tobacco related deaths has and is still one of the leading causes of preventable death worldwide. Many countries worldwide have introduced higher taxes on tobacco products to help reduce smoking rates. How effective are these taxes in reducing deaths caused by tobacco use such as smoking?

Tobacco taxes around the world vary depending on each country's policies. In many European countries and in Australia, taxes make up a significant amount of the price of cigarettes at around 70% or more. However, countries in Asia and Africa tend to have a much lower percentage of tax on the price of cigarettes.
